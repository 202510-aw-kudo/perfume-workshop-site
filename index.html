About

The fragrance experience

A calm, design-led workshop inspired by Japanese nature, refined materials, and the art of slow creation.

Concept

Crafted around Japanese sensibility

Inspired by nature and traditional craftsmanship, the workshop invites you to explore subtle accords, delicate balance, and a more personal approach to perfume.

Creation

Blend a scent that feels entirely yours

During the workshop, you will explore a variety of natural scents inspired by Japanese nature. With guidance from our staff, you will blend your own perfume and create a fragrance that reflects your personality. At the end of the experience, you will take home a bottle of your unique perfume as a special memory of Asakusa.

Process

A simple and elegant flow

01

Choose your notes

Discover a curated library of scents and identify the accords that resonate with you.

02

Blend with guidance

Adjust balance, depth, and character with support from our atelier team.

03

Take your bottle home

Leave with a finished fragrance that captures your visit in a tangible way.
